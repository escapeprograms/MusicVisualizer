About MusicSynth


Our Story

As musicians ourselves, we understand how tough it can be to either create or play a song or piece with the correct dynamics, feelings, etc. However, interpreting music in terms of real-life images or abstract visuals can truly bring out the correct amount of dynamics and feelings easily- that is why we created MusicSynth.

How MusicSynth Works

We provide sample midi files of music so you could see what images pop up after you use them. Users can also upload their own music midi files to see what visuals come up.
